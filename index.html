GeoCrowd is a location-based software designed to bring your social networking experience into real time. Anywhere in the world, you can post a note or a picture that automatically
geolocates your position and posts it to an interactive map for your friends to see. At any time in the day, you can turn on GeoCrowd to see where your friends are and what they’re doing on a map in REAL TIME.

GeoCrowd is an application that has its roots in disaster response. Utilizing similar location-based mobile software, First Responders are able to document field operations with time/date stamped and geocoded information in a completely disconnected environment. For more information about how applications such as GeoCrowd are utilized in disaster response operations, please visit www.pathfinderstaskforce.com
